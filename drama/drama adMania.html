Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Maximum time limit for each performance is 3-5 minutes.
  • There will be Negative marking for exceeding the time limit.
  • Team size 3-6 members.
  • Maximum 2 entries allowed per college.
  • Teams can use props of their choice.
  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
  • Use of abusive language in the performance will lead to disqualification.
  • Judgment criteria:
    • Creativity
    • Uniqueness
    • Script
    • Dialogue delivery
    • Overall performance
