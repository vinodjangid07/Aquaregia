Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • There are 3 rounds in total.
  • Round 1:Ramp walk
    Dress Code:
    • For Boys- Blazer, Tie and dress slacks or a suit with shoes.
    • For Girls- Cocktail Dress.
  • Round 2:Tell us about yourself (45-60 sec).
  • Round 3:Questions & Answer (45-60 sec to answer)
    • *Note:
    • Exceeding the time limit will lead to negative marking.
    • Certain number of participants will get eliminated at every round.
    • Participants can use both Hindi and English as mode of commmunication.

      • The winners will be titled as Mr. & Ms. Aquaregia .
    • Judgment Criteria:
      • Walk
      • Dress
      • Confidence
      • Communication/Question handling
      • Stage Presentation
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
  • The judges' decision will be final and binding.
