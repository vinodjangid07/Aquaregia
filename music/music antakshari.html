Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Each team will comprise of 3-4 members.
  • Only songs from Bollywood movies, Punjabi songs and Famous albums are allowed by default.
  • No song can be repeated in the game.
  • The decision of the judges shall be final and binding on all teams.
  • Team is expected to adhere to the timeline for each round.

Round 1: Elimination round Rules:

  • In this round a name of an actor will be provided to each team and the team must write maximum number of songs of the actor in the given time.
  • Different actors would be given to each team.
  • Time limit for this round is 1 minute.
  • Top 8 teams who will write the maximum number of songs will be selected for the next round.
  • The name of the song should be accurate.

Round 2: Buzzer Round Rules:

  • In this round music of a song will be played in the background and the teams have to guess the song as well as the movie name.
  • The team must sing at least 2 paragraphs of that song
  • Negative marking will be done in this round.
  • Top 4 teams will be selected at the end of this round based on their performance, whoever answers the question and sings the song with perfect lyrics.

Round 3: The Final Round Rules:

  • This round will be a normal Antakshari round. This means that the first song must start with(m)
  • Songs must be started from the last letter of the previous song.
  • 10 seconds will be given to each team to think of a song. Whichever team fails to sing a song in the given period of time will be eliminated directly.
  • At least the first verse of the song must be sung
  • Only songs from Bollywood movies, Hindi/Punjabi albums are allowed.
  • Participants must follow the original song, any modification/changes in the songs will lead to disqualification.
  • Judge's decisions will be final and cannot be changed.
