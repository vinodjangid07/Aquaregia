Rules & Regulations

General Rules:

  • Number of participants can be 1 or 2 (1 singer and 1 accompanist if required).
  • Participants can sing any song of their choice provided decency is maintained at all time.
  • The Preferred languages for songs are: - Hindi, Rajasthani, Punjabi and English.
  • The time limit for each performance will be 4 minutes.
  • Exceeding the time limit will lead to disqualification.
  • Participants should bring their own instruments if required.
  • Karaoke music is allowed.
  • Foul language should not be used.
  • Judging Criteria: Quality of voice, Sense of scale and beat, Quality of rendition Improvisation, Pronunciation of lyrics, Mood and expression, Overall impact.
  • Judges decision will be final and binding.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances by the authorities.
    • The event will be conducted in two rounds.
    • Round 1 - Elimination Round(Online)
    • Round 2 - Showcase Round

Round 1 Rules: Elimination Round(Online)

  • Photograph must be your own click.
  • The genres of the photograph will be (Street, Portrait, Fashion, Nature, Architecture, Night Exposure).
  • Photograph should not be less than 1 MB.
  • Only one photograph can be submitted by each participant.
  • Minor editing of light correction and color correction is allowed.
  • Photo should not be photoshopped or manipulated.
  • Any type of camera can be used. ( Digital SLRs, Mobile Camera, etc. )
  • There must be no border(s), logo(s), copyright marks, identifying marks, or any other visible references and/or marks on the image.
  • The photograph must be in JPEG/JPG format.
  • Judges decision will be final and binding.
  • You have to describe your picture in not more than 100 words.
    • Candidates should mail their entries on the e-Mail ID given below with the following details:-
    • Candidate's Name
    • Event Name
    • College Name
    • Course and year
    • Mobile Number
    • Description
    • Email ID - photographyaquaregia2022@gmail.com
  • Any Content that might be offensive to any culture/language/region/religion will lead to direct disqualification.
  • Last date of submission:- 6th February.
  • If the photograph is submitted later than the specified date and time the entry will be disqualified.
  • The winner's photo will be posted on the official Instagram handle of Aquaregia.
  • Judgment criteria: Creativity, Clarity of image, Description of photograph, Concept and uniqueness, Overall impact
  • The decision of judges will be final and binding.
  • Rules may get modulated under certain circumstances.
